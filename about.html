About

Hi, I'm Musa

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer who brings ideas to life and solves real-world problems through impactful design. With many years of experience, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from producing bold brand identities to delivering multi-channel initiatives for brands like BT, EE, and ITV. My approach combines creativity and strategy to deliver designs that not only look great, but also engage and resonate deeply with audiences.

I excel in creative direction, overseeing end-to-end creative processes, and collaborating across disciplines to push boundaries. Specialising in driving innovation through design, I conceptualise ideas, prototype seamless user experiences, and utilise a range of tools—including motion graphics—to deliver compelling design outcomes. Every project is purposefully crafted to inspire, connect, and create meaningful impact.

With a detail-oriented mindset and a relentless passion for creativity, I’m committed to exceeding expectations and bringing ambitious visions to life.
