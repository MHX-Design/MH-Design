Covid-19 Updates

During the coronavirus pandemic, I created animated explainer videos based on the latest guidance from UK government authorities to deliver timely and accurate information to ITV audiences across the UK. Working with tight same-day turnarounds, I developed a branded, vector-based style that ensured the content was clear, engaging, and trustworthy.

At a time when TV viewing figures were at their peak and the need for reliable information was critical, these videos kept the nation informed and reached millions of viewers across the regions.

Tools used: Sketching, Adobe After effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, Cinema 4D
