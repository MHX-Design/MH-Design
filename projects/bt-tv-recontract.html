BT TV Re-contracting

To encourage customers to recontract their TV packages, I designed a multi-channel, two-touchpoint campaign using personalised email and direct mail. Each communication highlighted the value of upgrading with a bold and fresh design. The campaign featured various package options, from Essential to VIP, showcasing partnership content that enhanced customer value across tiers.

As a result, weekly regrades increased from 167 to 706, and ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) degradation improved from -4.24 to -2.05, demonstrating the impact of a targeted, multi-channel approach in driving recontracting success.

Tools used: Figma, Cinema 4D, Adobe After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign

Emails

The design of emails in desktop and mobile format, VIP (touchpoint 1) and Big sports (touchpoint 2) packages.

Direct Mail

Final designs of direct mail, VIP (touchpoint 1) and Entertainment (touchpoint 2) packages.
