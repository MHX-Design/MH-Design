View from Stormont

I refreshed the visual identity of the UK programme View from Stormont, collaborating closely with production teams to develop a new title sequence, including new animation and transitions, along with updated imagery for the studio set screens. This rebrand modernised the programme's aesthetic, giving it a sharper and more dynamic look that resonated with audiences.

The enhanced visuals reinforced the political theme, boosting the programme's appeal and leading to a noticeable increase in viewer engagement.

Tools used: Adobe After Effects, Photoshop
