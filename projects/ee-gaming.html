EE Gaming

Campaigns designed to drive awareness into EE's gaming partnerships using innovative channels and methods to interact with customers.

Tools used: Figma, Cinema 4D, Adobe After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop

Starfield (Xbox)

A rich media campaign hosted on the Sinch platform, showcasing the launch of Starfield alongside other Xbox Game Pass titles and EE's ultrafast broadband plans. I created a cohesive and engaging user experience by designing the landing page, editing the promotional video, and producing all campaign visuals.

The campaign resulted in a 7.7% click-through rate and 88.4% engagement rate, highlighting strong audience interest and excitement.

Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth (Playstation)

Another rich media campaign, promoting the highly anticipated launch of Final Fantasy VII Rebirth exclusively on PlayStation 5. The campaign highlighted a PS5 Console bundle for EE customers. I designed the visuals, created the MMS, animated the engaging promotional video, and developed the landing page.

The campaign achieved a 7.2% click-through rate and 96.4% engagement rate, driving significant interest in the gaming package.

Call of duty Modern Warfare 3

An MMS and email campaign in partnership with Activision to engage EE gamers ahead of the game launch. Featuring striking visuals and action-driven messaging, it encouraged opt-ins for exclusive benefits and achieved strong engagement, reinforcing EE's connection to gaming audiences.
