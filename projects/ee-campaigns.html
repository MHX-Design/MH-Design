Leveraging MMS/SMS and Rich media, I designed a multi-channel awareness campaign to educate EE mobile customers about the benefits of Full Fibre Technology (FTTP). The campaign featured interactive elements, including a game and landing page, to increase engagement and highlight the speed, reliability, and versatility of FTTP broadband.
