Corporate Let

I transformed the kitchen, dining, and utility spaces of a house into a fully furnished, turn-key home. It was designed for corporate rental, ready to accommodate an executive and their family. The design process was guided by extensive user research and needs assessments to ensure the space meets the practical demands and lifestyle expectations of a professional audience.

Tools used: Sketching, 3dsMax, AutoCAD, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop

Concepting

I developed the design through detailed sketches, elevation and floor plans, including furniture layouts and a mood board. I focused on space planning by analysing user traffic flows, zoning, and applying universal standards to create a functional and visually appealing space.

Final Design

The final design includes detailed layout plans, a comprehensive lighting plan and 3D renders of the spaces to bring the vision to life.

A neutral colour palette and intuitive layout make the home universally appealing and easy to navigate. Combining style with functionality, the design creates a welcoming and adaptable environment that feels like home from day one. .
